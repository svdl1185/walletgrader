How the memecoin score works

  • Holding quality: higher median time between transactions scores better.
  • Activity shape: moderate activity is best; hyperactive behavior is reduced.
  • Churn: frequent in/out within short windows reduces the score.
  • Account age: contributes a small amount.
  • Balance: lightly weighted.
